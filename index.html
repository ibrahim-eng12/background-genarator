Our project

Our project is a robot that classifies waste and separates it according to its type into paper, plastic, metal, and organic waste through artificial intelligence and using the PICTOBLOX application. It runs on solar energy, cuts paper with blades, and turns food into fertilizer for the soil. There is also a program that explains the areas where these machines are located and how much they are filled, as well as how to use the machine in brief steps. There is also a website that shows other types of this machine and other details
